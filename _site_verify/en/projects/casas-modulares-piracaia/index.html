Modular houses in Piracaia

Piracaia, Brazil — 2024

Specifications

  • Location Piracaia, Brazil
  • Year 2024
  • Area 250 m²
  • Architects Arquipélago Arquitetos
  • Photographs Pedro Kok

Proposal for a modular housing construction system in rammed earth and wood, designed to be reproducible. The rammed earth walls function as structural masses that support the wooden roof panels. The aim was to question the limits and virtues of artisanal manual construction techniques and the universe of industrial production.
