Casa Quinta

Paraty, Brasil — 2024

Specifications

  • Location Paraty, Brasil
  • Year 2024
  • Area 190 m²
  • Architects Arquipélago Arquitetos
  • Photographs Pedro Kok

Located 100 meters above sea level, in the Caboclo neighborhood in Paraty, the house is immersed in a natural slope of the Atlantic Forest that extends to form the Serra da Bocaina. It is the last elevation where construction is permitted in the municipality… The constructive challenges led to a light architecture, in industrialized wood… Elevated from the ground, accessed by a walkway from the highest level, is the nave of the house, an image of a boat floating above the forest.
