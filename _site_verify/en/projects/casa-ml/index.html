Casa ML

Paraty, Brasil — 2016

Specifications

  • Location Paraty, Brasil

  • Area 140 m²
  • Architects Arquipélago Arquitetos
  • Photographs Pedro Napolitano Prata
  • Manufacturers Ananda Metais, Decorlit, Glasvale, Mato Dentro

Located in a hard-to-reach clearing in the Serra do Mar of Paraty, Rio de Janeiro… the house project was based on a dry construction strategy that provided comfort in a hot and humid environment. The constructive approach considered the use of light and prefabricated components.
