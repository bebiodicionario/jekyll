Cinema da Praça

Paraty, Brasil — 2018

Specifications

  • Location Paraty, Brasil
  • Year 2018
  • Area 475 m²
  • Architects Arquipélago Arquitetos
  • Photographs Pedro Napolitano Prata
  • Manufacturers Kastrup, Securit, reka iluminacao

The interior of the listed townhouse of the old cinema in the city of Paraty was in ruins… The architectural project is guided by the perception that the history of this listed building is not frozen in time, but open to its continuity as a living space… It seeks an intentional distinction between new elements and existing ones, avoiding confusion between the old and the new.
